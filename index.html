Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an official government service? ▼

No. We are a private service. We are not affiliated with any government agency. Our reports are for guidance and preparation only.

What types of documents can I send? ▼

You can send PDFs, photos (JPEG/PNG), and plain text. Our AI can read labels, menus, procedure manuals, and signage photos.

Is my data kept confidential? ▼

Absolutely. Your documents are processed entirely on your device using client-side OCR for free scans. They are never uploaded to any server. We never share or sell your data.

How long does it take? ▼

Most reports are delivered within 2 minutes. Complex documents may take up to 5 minutes.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept all major credit cards and digital wallets via our secure payment partner, Paddle. Subscriptions are billed monthly and can be cancelled at any time.
